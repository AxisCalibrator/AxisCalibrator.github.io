How to use
  1. Connect a supported gamepad and press a button.
  2. Leave sticks untouched and click "Measure resting drift" to detect drift.
  3. Use "Auto-calibrate" to estimate a recommended deadzone.
  4. Adjust the slider to set your preferred deadzone.
